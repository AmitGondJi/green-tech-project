About the Project

The Green Technology Awareness Platform is a project designed to educate students and educators about sustainable IT practices. With the growth of digital infrastructure, energy usage and e-waste generation have risen sharply. This platform bridges the knowledge gap by offering interactive tutorials, guides, and resources to promote eco-friendly computing in academic communities.

Problem Statement

  • • Limited knowledge of eco-friendly IT among students and faculty.
  • • Existing campaigns lack interactive engagement tools.
  • • Minimal understanding of e-waste disposal and recycling methods.

Objectives

  • • Build a responsive website for all devices.
  • • Provide tutorials & guides for Green IT adoption.
  • • Encourage sustainable digital habits through awareness & education.

Scope

The platform acts as a centralized digital hub to inspire behavior change, guide institutions on best practices, and promote eco-friendly computing across academic communities.
